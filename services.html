This is what we do

Web Development

 

Web Development broadly refers to the tasks associated with developing websites for hosting via the Internet. The web development process includes web design, web content development, client-side/server-side scripting and network security configuration, amongst others. At ThirtyEight Co, we provide a personal service, tailored to our client’s specific needs, ensuring their vision comes to life.

UI/UX Design

 

Although UI design and UX design are two separate services, they’re both extremely complementary of one another and even draw from each other.

 

User interface design (UI), or user interface engineering, is the design of user interfaces for machines and software, such as computers, home appliances, mobile devices, and other electronic devices, with deeper focus on maximizing usability and user experience. When crafting UI designs, we carefully think through the process in order to determine how certain decisions will affect the final product, with our ultimate goal to completely satisfy our own clients, and furthermore, our clients’ clients.

 

User experience design (UX), is the process of enhancing user satisfaction with a product by improving the usability, accessibility, and pleasure provided in the interaction with the product. By using UX design, our team are committed to transforming anything from initial ideas, to in-depth, detailed descriptions of a product in need of direction into the ultimate vision in your mind. We think critically about how your app/website will transport a user from screen to screen.

Business Branding

 

Branding is the marketing practice of creating a name, symbol or design that identifies and differentiates one product from another. Branding is a key element in putting across the benefits of your product or service to the customers. An effective brand strategy gives you a major edge in increasingly competitive market.

 

Within business branding, we include Graphic Design services. This encompasses numerous services such as Logo Design, Posters, Social Media Artwork and Business Cards. We have provided many of these services for our clients upon request, some of which you can view on our online Portfolio.
